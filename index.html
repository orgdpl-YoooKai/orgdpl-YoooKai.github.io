Greetings traveler! I am the webmaster or perhaps, the dungeon master of this realm.

I am a creature standing at 1.60m, hailing from a small island called Tenerife, in Spain. Many, including myself, refer to me as something of a hobbit. My heart longs for a quiet wooden cottage nestled in the mountains, where I can savor the peace, the comfort of familiar routines, and, of course, the joys of good food.

I studied Fine Arts, but my pursuit of perfection and relentless self-criticism kept me from dedicating myself fully to it. I've come to realize that making art my profession would extinguish the passion I hold for drawing, just as it did during my university years. So, my path remains unwritten, as I now study web development, a craft both challenging and captivating for me, even though my grasp on logic isn't strong. I press on because, well, I have four dogs and countless vet appointments to cover.

Though I am introverted, often finding solace in the company of my animals and the vastness of the internet, I also yearn to travel and live in different corners of the world. For now, my adventures take place in the worlds I explore through games and media, as my island sometimes feels too small for the exploring I'd like to do.

Life, for me, has been a series of quiet, mundane chapters, shaped by difficult seasons. While change unsettles me, I am learning to embrace it, because I wish to truly experience and savor what life has to offer. These last few years, I found myself drifting, operating on autopilot with an empty mind. But now, I feel the stirrings of movement, the slow yet steady march forward. Building this personal website has awakened a creative spark in me, pushing me to look within and rediscover what truly matters and how I feel.

Chaotic Good A chaotic good character acts as his conscience directs him with little regard for what others expect of him. He makes his own way, but he's kind and benevolent. Race:
Elves are known for their poetry, song, and magical arts, but when danger threatens they show great skill with weapons and strategy. Elves can live to be over 700 years old and, by human standards, are slow to make friends and enemies, and even slower to forget them. Elves are slim and stand 4.5 to 5.5 feet tall. They have no facial or body hair, prefer comfortable clothes, and possess unearthly grace. Many others races find them hauntingly beautiful.

Class:
Rangers are skilled stalkers and hunters who make their home in the woods. Their martial skill is nearly the equal of the fighter, but they lack the latter's dedication to the craft of fighting. Instead, the ranger focuses his skills and training on a specific enemy a type of creature he bears a vengeful grudge against and hunts above all others. Rangers often accept the role of protector, aiding those who live in or travel through the woods. His skills allow him to move quietly and stick to the shadows, especially in natural settings, and he also has special knowledge of certain types of creatures. Finally, an experienced ranger has such a tie to nature that he can actually draw on natural power to cast divine spells, much as a druid does, and like a druid he is often accompanied by animal companions. A ranger's Wisdom score should be high, as this determines the maximum spell level that he can cast.
